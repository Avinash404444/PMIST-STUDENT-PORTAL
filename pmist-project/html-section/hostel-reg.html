The Student has to register the requirement status (Yes/No) for the hostel accommodation. If “Yes”, he/she has to choose Room Type as Common, Non AC Double, Non AC Single, AC Double&AC Single. Based on the Room Type, the hostel fees will be posted for the respective student.

The Student has to register the requirement status (Yes/No) for the Bus facility. If “Yes”, he/she has to choose Bus &Bus Stop and accordingly the bus fees will be posted for the respective student.

The Student can pay the fees (University / Hostel / Bus / Others) through this link. The bill for the fees payment also generated. No need to approach the Institution Office for the bill generation. Before paying the fees, the Student has to register the requirement status of the Hostel and Bus.

The Student has to register for the Open Elective Courses of the forthcoming semester through this link.

The Student has to register for the Core and Elective Courses of the forthcoming semester through this link. The Student will be able to register only after the payment of the pending fees.

For any further queries & support regarding the online student registration, kindly contact the PMIST e-Governance Section @ 7708108726.
